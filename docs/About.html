About Me

UX Designer and HCI Researcher

Hi, I’m Esther Castro. I study Human-Computer Interaction at Uppsala University, building on a background in Systems and Computer Science from Stockholm University.

I care deeply about crafting digital experiences that are meaningful, accessible, and grounded in empathy. My design work often reflects a love for simplicity and clarity, with inspiration drawn from nature and everyday human moments.

I enjoy working through research and iteration to shape intuitive and inclusive interfaces. Whether I am mapping a journey, designing a prototype, or gathering user feedback, my goal is always to create with thoughtfulness and care.

Design Values

I believe good design begins with listening. Understanding people’s needs, goals, and emotions is at the heart of every meaningful solution. I value collaboration, curiosity, and kindness in the design process.

My approach is calm, human-centered, and focused on bringing clarity and ease into people’s lives. I believe in designing with purpose and with care, always looking for ways to make digital experiences more joyful and inclusive.

Skills and Tools

  • User research and interviews
  • Wireframing and prototyping in Figma and Adobe XD
  • Interaction and interface design
  • Usability testing
  • Accessibility-focused design
  • Workshop facilitation
  • Design thinking methods
  • Generative and creative AI tools
